Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2024

Introduction

The TickTock Timer application ("app") takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects your data.

Data Collection

The TickTock Timer app respects user privacy and follows the principle of minimal data collection. Our app:

  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not collect user identity
  • Does not use third-party analytics tools

Local Data Storage

The app only stores the following settings locally on your device:

  • Theme preference
  • Language preference
  • Sound effects setting
  • Seconds display setting

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Permissions

Our app does not require any special permissions to function. The app:

  • Does not require internet access
  • Does not require camera access
  • Does not require microphone access
  • Does not require location access
  • Does not require contacts access

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our app and data has been collected, please contact us.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you within the app.
